Activities

Women’s Ministry Programme

In addition, the Seminary provides a Women’s Ministry Programme for students’ wives who do not qualify to enter one of the other programmes of study. The sessions are weeklong, and are normally conducted three times each year. The emphasis is on practical skills and basic Bible studies that can help each of the women in her ministry within the home and church. These include healthcare, the Gospel and culture and women’s work.

Supervised Field Ministry Experience and Learning Contracts

Baptist Theological Seminary of Malawi seeks to train for effective service those whom God has called. Therefore, Supervised Field Ministry Experience (SME) AND Learning Contracts (LC) is a must for students in all programmes except for the Wives Ministry programme because it is an itegral part of the training of every student enrolled at this Seminary. The student may not graduate withoug completing the SME and LC. Through the Field Ministry Experience and Learning Contracts, Baptist Theological Seminary of Malawi attempts to bridge the gap between the acquisition of knowledge and imparting and translating that knowledge into relevant practical skills.
