Diploma in Theology

The Diploma in Theology is a post-secondary programme of study. It is a three-year prograomme validated by the University of Malawi (UNIMA). The programme is designed to meet the needs of those who are in training for the ministry of the churches. The life, witness and service of the churches in Malawi provide the context whithin which all the courses are studied.
